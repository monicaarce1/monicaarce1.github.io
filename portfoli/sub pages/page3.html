Glitch Project


For this concept, I used a traveling method of traveling from New York (Top) to San Francisco (Bottom). The meaning of this concept is because of how these two cities are a big connection to technology. The first photo I used Notepad ++. I went through the codes and copied and pasted some of the codes and I took out lines of code to create this image of broken buildings, which looks like inceptions on through the buildings.
The second photo I used Audacity to make this glitch. The effects that I used were compressor and phaser. I played around with both effects, which came to be the image below. I like this effect because of how it made the photo very glitchy and the colors compliment each other.
