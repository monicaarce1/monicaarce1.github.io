3D Sculpture


The cultural meaning of this work is all about anime culture. Anime is one of the biggest cultures in society because of how it brings joy and entertainment to people. My model of No Face is one of the examples of anime because of how it relates to one of my favorite animated movies. For this project we used a series of programs like mineways and cura to complete the sculpture. Working through Minecraft and using programs to print the 3D sculpture made it possible to create two different version of the sculpture and comparing it to each other.
