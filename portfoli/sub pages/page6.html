Self Portrait


This is a self portrait of myself. I used Processing to create this project. Most of the shapes that I used inmyself portrait is mostly circles and rectangles. Just by looking at the way I describe myself is more chill and relaxed. I made that possible with mostly the eyes and the hair covering my face. With my smile I wanted a more relaxed tone. It may look like i'm saying "hey" or "welcome." I put a shadow under where my neck area is at because I wanted that depth look to define more of my face. I also incorporated my beauty marks in a more depth, but low profile. I incorporated that in my self portrait because it defines of who am I and not leave anything behind since it's an important feature on my face.
